Chapter 01

How to
sleep better

Scroll to drift through the dream-space. Each tip surfaces as you traverse.

  • 01 — First tip
    🌗 Pre-bed ritual
    Dim the lights at sunset. Allow your circadian rhythm to slip into rest mode naturally — no screens after 10pm.
  • 02 — Second tip
    🍵 Warm a cup
    Caffeine-free herbal tea triggers the body's cooling reflex on the way down — ideal 60 minutes before sleep.
  • 03 — Third tip
    🌬 Breath of four
    Inhale four counts, hold four, exhale four, hold four. Box breathing slows the heart and quiets the mind.
  • 04 — Fourth tip
    📓 Empty the brain
    Three lines of tomorrow's worries on paper before bed. Externalised tasks stop looping in REM.
  • 05 — Fifth tip
    🌡 Cool the room
    A bedroom around 18°C lets your core temperature drop in sync with melatonin release.
  • 06 — Sixth tip
    🌙 Same time tomorrow
    Wake at the same hour every day — even weekends. Consistency is the deepest sleep hack.
